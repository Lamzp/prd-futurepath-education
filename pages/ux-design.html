ABOUT THIS PROGRAMME

Our UX Design summer school offers students a taster of what it’s like to have a career in UX design in a carefully designed programme taught by expert tutors. It’s a unique opportunity to study a subject at industry-level, exploring opportunities in the game design sector such as web and app design, and gaining an insight into some of the essential theoretical and practical foundations of this creative field. Expert tutors will introduce you to fundamental UX design concepts, and will also develop prototyping & wireframing balanced with creative and conceptual skills.
