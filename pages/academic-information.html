ACADEMIC INFORMATION

Learn more about how we impart our world-class educational model to driven students from all over the world. Continue reading to discover more about our leading education, challenging academic programs, cutting-edge tutorials, and personalized participant evaluations.

01. Expert Tutors

Tutors Specialised In Their Respective Fields

In small groups of like-minded peers, participants will explore unfamiliar concepts and participate in discussions. They will receive guidance from experienced tutors who have polished their expertise by educating undergraduate students at leading colleges including Imperial College London, Brunel University London, and University of the Arts London.

All participants are taught by instructors who are passionate about sharing their knowledge, engaging, and encouraging. This assures that the summer school programme is of the best quality.

Tutors Specialised In Their Respective Fields

02. Push Yourself Further

Well Designed and Exciting Courses

Participants in Futurepath Education's educational programmes have the unique chance to study the field they are most enthusiastic about at a university level through courses that are developed and delivered by knowledgeable and experienced teachers.

Participants are challenged to push themselves beyond the constraints of a school curriculum and rise to the next level by exploring the newest exciting and challenging content. Our academic programs differentiate themselves due to the modern technology & creative courses not commonly explored in standard education - opening new doors for students and providing the insight needed for participants to confidently take that next step into the future.

03. Careful Consideration

A Personal Touch

Our small class sizes encourage interactive learning, participation, and discussion as participants tackle technically challenging topics. There are typically 9 students (with a maximum of 15). Each academic session, every student's individual aptitude is taken into consideration. Additional tutorials provide students the chance to be introduced to methods to fully personalize their study.

A Personal Touch

04. A Bridge Towards The Future

Well Designed and Exciting Courses

All Futurepath participants, ages 15 to 18, will complete a personal project in their individual courses with the assistance of professional tutors. Each participant has the opportunity to develop their unique research, industry-related, and communication skills through the personal project..

These projects will help students build both essential soft skills that are transferable to success in higher education or their careers, as well as hard skills that are commonly applied in industry. We would love to see our students welcome the chance to research a subject that interests them and find their passion through Futurepath. Throughout the whole program, tutors make care to offer tailored guidance on each project. Some participants take advantage of the chance to develop their ideas into something even bigger, such as a business idea.

05. Personalised Feedback

Participant Evaluation

Our tutors care deeply about their students' academic growth, and they offer personalized written feedback and reflections following the program. This offers insight into a participant's skills, potential research areas, and suggested courses of action to spark their passion and curiosity.

Participant Evaluation

06. The Future & Beyond

Imparting A Mindset For The Future

We strive to inspire confidence in each of our participants since we know it's crucial to the learning process. We do this b y equipping our students with the knowledge and skills they need to reach their full potential. Futurepath Education's academic programs are interactive, challenging, and vibrant. In order to be exposed to newer perspectives, students explore concepts within realistic circumstances and participate in hands-on skill-building activities.

We strongly encourage native and competent level speakers to enrol in our programs as well as non-native speakers to develop their English language skills through content and language-integrated learning.
