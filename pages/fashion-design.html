FASHION DESIGN

Futurepath Summer School Programme

ABOUT THIS PROGRAMME

If you love fashion and clothing, you are already 1 step into the world of fashion design. A fashion designer may be able to create the next season’s featured piece for top luxury brands, start their own clothing brands, be your favourite celebrity’s stylist, and so much more!

Enrolling in the fashion design course gives you that extra step into the fashion industry - be it abstract luxury fashion or plain and simple aethetic utility fashion. Fashion design at Futurepath will show you your future in fashion from studying the course in university to your potential careers and day in the life of a fashion designer.

You will learn the basics of fashion design, the theory of textiles and model prototyping, speak with designers currently in the fashion industry who have designed pieces for luxury brands, and most importantly - understand what fashion design entails and if it is right for you!
